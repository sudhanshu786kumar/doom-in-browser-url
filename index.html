URL Doom 1993

Play the original Doom vibe inside the URL bar with a grid of Braille cells.

Controls
  • Move: `WASD` or arrow keys
  • Fire: `Space` (pistol/shotgun)
  • Switch weapon: `1` pistol, `2` shotgun
  • Ability: `Q` (small AOE nearby)
  • Toggle render: `R` (top-down vs raycast)
Goal
  • Find the `Key` then step on the `Exit` to go to the next level.
  • Pick up `Medkit` to heal, `Armor` to reduce damage, `Shells` for shotgun ammo.
URL HUD

The URL shows the grid and status: `[hp] [ar] [wp] [sh] [ky] [lvl] [kills]`.
